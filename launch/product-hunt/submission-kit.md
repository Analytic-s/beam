## Suggested Replies / Objection Handling

1. "How is this different from Plausible/Fathom/GA?"
- Beam is positioned around decision support (channels, goals, events, change detection) rather than only passive traffic totals.
- We keep the setup and pricing simple for indie makers and small teams.

2. "Is this compliant if I care about privacy?"
- Beam tracking is cookie-free and avoids storing personal identifiers.
- It is designed to reduce consent-banner burden while preserving useful aggregate analytics.

3. "Why not use Google Analytics for free?"
- Teams that choose Beam usually want simpler UX, privacy-first defaults, and faster signal-to-action loops.
- Beam intentionally avoids enterprise-level attribution complexity.

4. "Is there a free tier?"
- Yes: free plan includes 1 site and up to 50K pageviews/month.
- Pro is $5/month for unlimited sites and 500K pageviews/month.

5. "Can I migrate from my current analytics tool?"
- Yes. Beam has migration guidance and scanner tooling for common setups.
- Start here: `https://beam-privacy.com/migrate?ref=product-hunt&utm_source=producthunt&utm_medium=launch&utm_campaign=ph_launch_apr_2026`
