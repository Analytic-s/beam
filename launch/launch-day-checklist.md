## 2) Launch-Day Steps

- Open the platform submission URL from each template file.
- Paste the suggested title and body.
- Keep the tracked campaign URL unchanged.
- Submit and save each live post URL to `progress.txt`.

## 3) First Metrics To Check (first 30-60 minutes)

- `/dashboard/acquisition`:
  verify lift in `first_touch_source`, `first_touch_campaign`, and signup cohorts.
- `/dashboard/sites/:id/analytics?range=7d`:
  verify pageview and referrer movement by channel/source.
- `/dashboard/sites/:id/analytics?range=today`:
  verify same-day traffic spike and top landing paths.

## 4) Fast Response Loop

- Reply quickly to early questions/comments.
- Note repeated objections/confusion for follow-up copy updates.
- Keep mirrored Show HN files in sync if wording or URLs are changed.
